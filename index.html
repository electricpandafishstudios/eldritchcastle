What is The Eldritch Castle?

The Eldritch Castle is the first official game being developed by ElectricPandaFish Studios. A roguelike, programmed in the ToME 4 Engine; The Eldritch Castle is a Lovecraft inspired Horror game about insanity and hallucination.

Unfortunately, That scale of that project was too large of a project for our studio to handle; and so we put The Eldritch Castle on the back-burner, and began work on a similar Lovecraftian roguelike.

Our new project: The Valley of Nis, takes a more direct page from Lovecraft's book. Specifically, the page that has his prose-poem memory on it. Featuring character degradation, rather than character growth; The Valley of Nis is designed with speed in mind, as the longer one grinds an area, the weaker one will become...
